Welcome — this is a test version.

We are testing how well this AI understands young people's questions and gives responses in selected Ghanaian languages on Adolescent Sexual and Reproductive Health (ASRH), including topics such as growing up, changes in your body, feelings, and making healthy choices.

This platform is for testing the AI only. It is not designed to give personal health advice, counselling, diagnosis, or treatment. The AI may sometimes give answers that are incomplete, not fully correct, or not suitable for your situation.

Please use the answers as general information only. For personal, serious, or urgent concerns, please speak to a health worker, counsellor, trusted adult, or contact SHEplus Ghana: 055 054 5672 / 0800 00 11 22.

To protect yourself, please do not share personal details such as your full name, phone number, address, school, exact location, or anything that could identify you.

By using this platform, you are helping us improve the AI so it can understand questions and give safer, clearer, and more helpful responses in the future.
